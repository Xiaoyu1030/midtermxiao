Welcome to Ukulele Scales!

Scales are available for three popular tunings. The default tuning is GCEA (standard or C tuning) but ADF#B (soprano or D tuning) and DGBE (baritone tuning) are available as quick

tunings.

After you have selected your tuning you need to first select a root note or tonic. The final step is

to then select one of the many ukulele scales available.
